Who We Are

A Northshore Company Built on Integrity

Cypress Gulf Coast Roofing & Gutters was founded right here in Covington with a simple mission: bring big-city expertise and small-town accountability to every roof we touch. We're not a national franchise or a storm-chaser outfit that rolls in after a hurricane and disappears — we're your neighbors.

We've watched South Louisiana grow, we've weathered its storms, and we've built our business one trusted referral at a time. When you call Cypress Gulf Coast, you're calling a team that's as committed to this community as you are.

Based at 1331 Ochsner Blvd in Covington, we serve the entire Northshore, Southshore, Gulf Coast, and Capital Region — bringing the same level of professionalism and care to every job, regardless of size.
