FORTIFIED Roofing: How to Cut Your Insurance Bill and Protect Your Louisiana Home

If you haven't heard about the IBHS FORTIFIED Home™ program, you're leaving money on the table. Louisiana homeowners with FORTIFIED-certified roofs qualify for substantial discounts on homeowner's insurance — in some cases reducing annual premiums by hundreds of dollars.

Best Roofing Materials for South Louisiana's Climate: Asphalt vs. Metal vs. FORTIFIED

Choosing the right roofing material for a Louisiana home isn't the same as choosing for Colorado or New England. Our unique climate — extreme UV radiation, humidity that rarely drops below 70%, tropical storms, and hail — demands different performance characteristics than most parts of the country.

We compare architectural asphalt shingles, standing seam metal, and FORTIFIED-designated systems on cost, performance, longevity, and insurance impact — so you can make the best decision for your home and budget.
