FORTIFIED Certified

IBHS FORTIFIED Roofing

The IBHS FORTIFIED Home™ program sets the gold standard for wind and hail resistance in the Gulf Coast region. As a certified FORTIFIED roofer, we install systems that meet the rigorous standards required for this designation — and we handle the certification paperwork for you.

Why FORTIFIED Matters in Louisiana

  • Qualify for significant homeowner's insurance discounts
  • Engineered to withstand 130+ mph wind events
  • Third-party certified — insurers trust the designation
  • Increases your home's resale value

Gutters

Gutter Systems & Leaf Protection

Louisiana averages over 60 inches of rain per year — one of the highest in the nation. Without proper gutters, that water pools against your foundation, erodes landscaping, and causes interior damage. We install seamless gutters engineered for heavy Gulf Coast rainfall.

  • 6" K-style seamless aluminum gutters
  • LeafGuard & micro-mesh gutter protection
  • Copper gutters for premium curb appeal
  • Downspout extensions & drainage solutions
